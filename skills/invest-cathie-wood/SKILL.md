- **Exponential Growth:** Focuses on adoption curves and massive Total Addressable Market (TAM).

- **R&D Intensity:** High investment in research as a measure of innovation commitment.

## Key Metrics
- **Revenue Growth:** Accelerating growth (e.g. >20-50% YoY).
- **R&D Intensity:** R&D expense as % of revenue (prefer >15%).
- **Gross Margin:** Expanding trends (suggests scalability/pricing power).
- **Operating Leverage:** Revenue growing faster than expenses.
- **Capital Allocation:** Reinvestment over dividends.
